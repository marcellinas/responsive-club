About Us

We love and grow hardy perennials for their variety, colours, shapes, sizes and longevity. We help each other to grow them better, to try different species, and we exchange and spread information about them.

Being members of the Hardy Plant Society gives us more opportunities to meet others, in local groups and special interest groups focusing on a particular plant family or growing conditions; to attend National Society events; to obtain a wide range of perennial plants and take part in our Seed Distribution Scheme and, by being involved in our Conservation Scheme, help keep garden-worthy plants in cultivation by as many people as possible.

Our group meets at Shirland Village Hall in the village of Shirland near Alfreton. We usually meet up at 2pm on the 3rd Saturday of the month from September to April (except in December, which is often earlier).

If you’re interested in joining our group, please contact our Secretary – Celia Weaver – email: derby@hardy-plant.org.uk
